About Us

Our story and creative journey

ALFREDO ART AND DESIGN

Located in the serene hills of Atibie Kwahu, we are a creative studio dedicated to the art of pencil portraiture, custom picture framing, and graphic design.

With over a decade of experience, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design principles to create pieces that tell stories and capture moments.

Every project is approached with passion, precision, and attention to the smallest details.
